About the Platform

The Ghana Legal Timber Market (GLTM) is designed to facilitate ease of purchasing legal timber in Ghana. The platform is free and links interested buyers to sellers of legal wood. The platform allows customers to search for timber based on species, location and sizes.

Development Partners

This timber trade platform was developed by Nature and Development Foundation (NDF) in close collaboration with the Timber Industry Development Division (TIDD) of the Forestry Commission (FC). The platform is an output of the project, “Building Capacities of Small- Medium Forest Enterprises (SMES) in Ghana and Liberia to Supply and Trade in Legal Timber” The project was funded with UK aid from the British People.
